# SOCA — Version 1 (MVP) Scope & Expectations

Source: SOCA_SCOPE.docx (project scope document).  
This file is the canonical scope contract.

---

## 1. Project Objective
Launch a functional football-focused social platform that enables:
- Players to showcase talent
- Scouts and clubs to discover players
- All users to interact through profiles, content, challenges, and messaging

---

## 2. Core User Roles
SOCA V1 supports account types:
- Player
- Scout
- Club
- Organization (federations, leagues, media brands, governing bodies)

Roles determine permissions and visibility within a single unified platform.

---

## 3. Authentication & Onboarding
- Email & password sign-up
- Google sign-in
- Role selection during onboarding
- Profile setup flow per role

---

## 4. Profiles & Social Features

### Profile Features
- Profile photo & bio
- Location, role, and football-specific metadata
- Public profile pages
- Follower / following system
- Engagement metrics (views, likes)

### Content
- Video posts (highlights)
- Public feed visibility
- Like and view tracking

---

## 5. Video Upload & Playback
- Video upload for highlight content
- Secure, streamed video playback
- Basic metadata storage (duration, upload date)

---

## 6. Discovery & Explore

### Player Discovery
- Search and filtering by position, age, location, and role
- Sorted discovery views (latest, featured, popular)

### Explore Page
- Featured players
- Trending videos
- Challenges
- Events

---

## 7. Challenges & Leaderboards
- Admin-created monthly challenges (e.g. Top Dribble of the Month)
- Player video submissions
- Scoring based on admin input and engagement metrics
- Ranked public leaderboards

---

## 8. Messaging
- One-to-one text messaging between users
- Conversation list with unread indicators

---

## 9. Events
- Event listings (trials, showcases, scouting events)
- Event cards displayed on Home / Explore
- Event detail pages (date, location, description, organizer)
- Basic interaction (View / Interested)

---

## 10. AI Features (Assistive Intelligence)

SOCA V1 includes an assistive intelligence layer focused on improving discovery, relevance, and matching.

### A. Intelligent Discovery & Recommendations
- Rule-based and behavior-driven recommendation logic
- Ranking influenced by player profile attributes, scout preferences, and engagement signals

### B. Personalized Feeds
- Role-aware discovery feeds
- Scout feeds adapt based on interaction patterns
- Player feeds highlight relevant challenges and exposure opportunities

### C. Engagement-Based Ranking Signals
- Aggregation of scout interactions, shortlist activity, messaging interest, and challenge performance

---

## 11. Admin & Moderation
- Admin management of challenges
- Ability to feature content
- Moderation of reported content
- Manual verification badges (if required)

---

## 12. Project Deliverables
Primary deliverable:
- Complete mobile application source code (private GitHub repo)
- Basic setup documentation to run/extend/maintain

---

## 13. Demonstration & Review
- Recorded video walkthrough demonstrating core functionality

---

## 14. Out of Scope Items

### 14.1 Application Deployment & Distribution
Not included:
- App Store / Google Play / TestFlight publishing
- Distribution pipelines and compliance/review cycles

### 14.2 Hosting, Infrastructure & Ongoing Operations
Not included:
- Ongoing hosting/ops/monitoring/analytics
- Post-delivery operational support

---

## 15. Ownership & Responsibility

### 15.1 Account Ownership & Access
- Third-party service accounts are owned by the Client
- Developer may have temporary collaborator access; relinquish at completion

### 15.2 Post-Delivery Enhancements
- Additional features, web version, deployment assistance, continued dev => separate scope

---

## 16. Future Phases (Optional Roadmap)

### Phase 2 – Social, Video & Launch Expansion
- Stories / short-form reels
- Live streaming
- Enhanced moderation
- Push notifications
- Media in chat
- Multi-role account switching
- Event ticketing/RSVP/monetization
- Optional deployment support

### Phase 3 – Advanced Intelligence & Platform Expansion
- Web platform
- AI player comparison & ranking
- Scout preference learning ("Scout Fit Score")
- Computer vision performance analysis
- Live match analysis
- Advanced analytics dashboards
- Payments/contracts/transfers/agent management

---

## 17. Final Acceptance
Project is complete upon:
- Delivery of source code + demo video
- Confirmation implemented features align with agreed MVP scope
